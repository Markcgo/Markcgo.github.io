About me and this website.

I am currently a graduate student interested in AI, machine learning, optoelectronic materials, investing, and long-form thinking.

Who I am

I am He Kefei. My current academic background is connected with optoelectronic materials and characterization, while my long-term interests are expanding toward AI, machine learning, scientific computing, investing, language, and writing.

I use this website as a public notebook and a personal archive. Instead of letting learning remain scattered, I want to turn what I read, ask, test, and think into reusable notes, essays, and projects.

What I care about

My current focus includes AI, investment, research, language, and books. These are not separate hobbies for me. I want to connect them into a broader system of worldly wisdom.

The goal is not to collect information, but to build a durable knowledge system that can support research, writing, decision-making, and long-term personal growth.
